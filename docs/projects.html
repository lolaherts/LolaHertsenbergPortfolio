Marketing Initiative for Bay Law Injury Attorneys

Overview

As part of a marketing strategy course, my team conducted a marketing analysis for Bay Law Injury Attorneys, a personal injury law firm serving the Henderson and Las Vegas area. We evaluated the firm’s current marketing efforts, identified strengths and opportunities for improvement, and developed a year-long promotional strategy designed to increase brand awareness, customer engagement, and lead generation.

My Contributions

For this project, I was responsible for developing the Promotional Strategy section of the presentation. My work included researching digital and traditional marketing tactics, recommending social media and SEO initiatives, and creating a phased implementation plan to support long-term business growth.
